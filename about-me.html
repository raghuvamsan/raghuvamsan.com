How did I ended up as an Entrepreneur?

Here is a small info about myself and how did I ended up as an entrepreneur..

I'm a typical south Indian Computer Engineer who had lot of dreams during college of starting my own IT company and ending up successfully soon, thus becoming a rich guy who can afford sport bikes and cars and so on (a dream that any typical south Indian engineer would have).

So what happens to all those guys when they come out of their college with a bachelor or master degree and a decent score. We end up attending campus interviews in college and somehow manage to enter into any one of the several billion dollar revenue companies. Though we never thought of working for any company in the past years, we would be enjoying that we have an appointment order in our hands.

At this point all the dream that we had in our minds for the past several years of starting a company, growing our business, becoming an entrepreneur etc. would vanish. Same way I got my bachelors degree with a decent score and ended up in a several billion dollar revenue company. The big turning point in my life where I totally got screwed, lost all my talents that I developed during my college days to become an entrepreneur.

Almost 50% of the guys working in an IT company would have the same story like mine. But I couldn't just sit in the same job for long. After three years of shit (yes, I call it nothing but shit. Working in a MNC) I couldn't resist. So here I am, resigned my job and started of my own company http://atsquarelabs.com there by achieving my long lost dream of 'becoming an entrepreneur'.
